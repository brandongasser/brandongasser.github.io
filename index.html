My name is Brandon Gasser. I am a computer science student at North Dakota State University. I currently work at Data Recognition Corporation as a software development intern.

I am a member of the NDSU student chapter of the ACM. In the ACM, I lead a special interest group on functional programming, which is taught in Haskell. I also served as the Vice-Chair of the NDSU chapter of the ACM during the 2023-2024 academic year.

I am also a member of Bison Robotics at NDSU. I work on the Autonomous Snowplow and NASA Lunabotics projects. I am also a co-project lead for the Robot in 3 Days project in Bison Robotics.

In addition to my projects in Bison Robotics, I am involved in FIRST Robotics Competition. I volunteer at competitions as a control system advisor as well as mentoring a team.
